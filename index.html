Biography

I am a quantum information theorist, currently an assistant professor at the University of Hong Kong.
Before that (2018-21), I worked at ITP, ETH Zürich (Prof. Dr. Renato Renner's group) as a postdoc.
I obtained a PhD in Computer Science from the University of Hong Kong (2018) and a Bachelor in Physics from Tsinghua University (2013).

My research interests include:

  • Quantum Metrology: ultra-precise estimation using quantumness
  • Quantum Information: entropies, communication, and beyond
  • Quantum Computing: fundamental requirements on implementing quantum computation
  • Quantum Foundations: the interplay between information and laws of the universe

I am looking for motivated Ph.D. students.
Please contact me with your CV, publications (if any), transcript, and a brief statement of interest.

Several postdoc positions are now available in my group at the University of Hong Kong.
The candidate should have research experience in quantum metrology, quantum information, or quantum computing.
Please email me your CV, a brief research statement, representative (preferably first-author) publications, and at least one academic reference.
